## Description

The **Patient Emergency Visit Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze emergency department visit patterns, patient demographics, referral distribution, satisfaction levels, and operational performance metrics.

This dashboard provides healthcare stakeholders with data-driven insights to improve patient flow, reduce wait times, and enhance service quality.

---

## Overview

The dashboard includes the following key performance indicators (KPIs):

- **Total Patients:** 9,216  
- **Average Wait Time:** 35.26 minutes  
- **% Referred Patients:** 41.41%  
- **% Not Referred Patients:** 58.59%  
- **Average Satisfaction Rate:** 5.5  
- **Administrative vs Non-Administrative Split:** ~50/50  

### Visual Components

1. **Patient Visit by Week Type**
   - Weekday: 6.6K visits  
   - Weekend: 2.6K visits  

2. **Department Referral Distribution**
   - None: 5.4K  
   - General Practice: 1.8K  
   - Orthopedics: 1.0K  
   - Physiotherapy, Cardiology, Neurology, Gastroenterology, Renal (smaller distributions)

3. **Yearly Trend**
   - 2019: 4,338 visits  
   - 2020: 4,878 visits  
   - Demonstrates growth in emergency visits.

4. **Patient Demographics**
   - Adults represent the largest age group (7.1K visits)
   - Gender Split:
     - Male: 51.05%
     - Female: 48.69%

5. **Monthly Trend Analysis**
   - Increased visits from April to October
   - Drop observed in November and December

6. **Satisfaction Heatmap**
   - Displays satisfaction rate across race and age buckets
   - Darker green indicates higher satisfaction

---

## Results / Key Findings

- **Weekdays account for nearly 72% of total visits**, indicating higher operational demand during business days.
- **58.59% of patients were not referred**, suggesting most cases were resolved within emergency care.
- **Adults dominate emergency visits**, making them the primary target group for process optimization.
- **Average wait time of 35.26 minutes** may impact satisfaction and operational efficiency.
-  **Year-over-year growth** in visits suggests increasing healthcare demand.
- Satisfaction levels vary across age and race categories, highlighting potential service equity considerations.

---

## Technical Implementation

- **Tool Used:** Microsoft Power BI
- **Data Model:** Structured dataset with patient demographics, visit types, referral departments, satisfaction scores, and timestamps.
- **Visualizations Used:**
  - KPI Cards
  - Bar Charts
  - Line Charts
  - Heatmaps (Matrix with conditional formatting)
  - Slicers (AM/PM toggle)

---

## Conclusions

This dashboard reveals:

- Operational strain occurs primarily on weekdays.
- Adults represent the highest emergency visit demographic.
- A significant portion of cases are handled without referral.
- There is measurable annual growth in patient visits.
- Satisfaction insights can guide targeted service improvements.

The insights provided by this dashboard can support:
- Resource allocation planning
- Workforce scheduling
- Department capacity planning
- Quality improvement initiatives
